Mission

Our Mission

We started trading of coal directly from Coal India and is now supplying to various multinational companies and PSU divisions. Our company is in the business of import, export, distributor, dealer, stock yard operations, coal depot operations, wholesale, imported coal and retail trade in all type of coal, including hard coke, soft coke or industrial cake in all forms and petroleum products. Company caters to the requirement of Coking & Non-Coking coal of all grades from all major collieries, importers & other traders for onward supplies to the large corporates & PSU.
