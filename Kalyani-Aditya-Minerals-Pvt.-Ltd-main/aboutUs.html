About Us

Kalyani Aditya Minerals Private Limited was founded in the year 2010. It is a professional organisation owned and headed by the most experienced businessmen engaged in the coal trade for the last 25+ years. Under the guidance and supervision of Shri Shyam Lal Mittal Director, a leading name in this trade, the company progressed since its inception. The company's operations are now managed successfully by his son Mr. Saurabh Mittal and and daughter in law Mrs. Kriti Mittal. Our goal, ambition and inspiration have come together to form a truly amazing company that all of us are proud to be a part of.
